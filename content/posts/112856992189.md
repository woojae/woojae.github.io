Comedian, writer, actor and producer Larry David has made a career out of saying what you’d really like to say if you just had the chutzpah to say it. Inappropriate, socially awkward and laugh-out-loud hilarious, he has mastered telling a story about — nothing — while touching upon everything under the sun that could go wrong in the social engagements of life.
