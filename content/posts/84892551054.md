> My job involves me getting up WAY too early in the morning (think 4-6am) every day, and sometimes I’m wide awake when I know I need to sleep. I found this hack and decided to give it a shot. I drank a little less than what was indicated in the above picture and went from “AWAKE FOREVER” to “Alright, if I close my eyes I should be getting to sleep soon” within about 2-3 minutes. 
> 
> Apparently the reason it works is the [melatonin ](<http://www.webmd.com/sleep-disorders/tc/melatonin-overview>)in the Neuro Sleep.
> 
> So, here are my warnings:
> 
>   * Apparently people think you need to drink the entire bottle to get the effects. **NO**. At most drink about what this girl did in the picture.
>   * Almost a reiteration of above: just like all good things, [don’t overuse it. ](<http://www.koco.com/New-Neuro-Drinks-Raising-Health-Concerns/10737564>)If you find yourself going through more than a bottle a week, you’re probably overusing it.
> 

> 
> Tips on usage:
> 
>   * If you are in that state of tired where your brain won’t shut down, you know you’re exhausted, but can’t nap or switch off, a small sip is all you need to get to nappy mode.
>   * If it’s just about getting to sleep quickly, maybe a cup (the measurement, not just a random cup in your cupboard) should be enough to hit you hard and fast.
>   * Don’t take it if you need to wake up quickly in a couple of hours though. It will fuck you up, as a friend noted when taking a sip in the morning.
